Data

1) Question: How long are people homeless for?

Answer: More than 80% of people who are homeless, have been homeless for more than 1-2 years!

2) Question: How much do you live off a week?

Answer: Less than £30 a week!

3) Question: How much do you get from the public?

Answer: About £150 a month!

4) Question: How many meals do you eat a day?

Answer: A "meal" a day!

5) Question: How many clothes do you own?

Answer: All the clothes they would be currently wearing!

6) Question: What do you spend your money on?

Answer: Food, Newspaper, Drinks and Toiletries!

7) Question: How often do homeless people move?

Answer: Everyday!
